Welcome

Safety Guide for Rio de Janeiro

Vidigal is a small peaceful pacified community, on the hill of Dois Irmaos , with amazing views of Rio de Janeiro.

It is the first community that became a hot tourist destination, not least because of the famous hike in a national park up the top of the hill.

It is a relatively easy hike of about 30-40 minutes, if you start at the entrance point.

To go there you can take a motortaxi, or minivan (10 real / person).

You can also walk up from the bottom of the hill, Praça do Vidigal , which is considerably harder, doubling the time to the top of the mountain.

Personally, I prefer going like this, the path is easy to find, there is only one main road, just follow it, and when in doubt ask one of the locals, who are all friendly and accustomed to tourists.

Entrance fee to the hike is 10 real / person.

It is really worth it to start the hike when it is still dark, so you can enjoy the most spectacular sunrise of your life.

The views from up there are truly stunning - at any time - it is the best viewpoint of Rio.

Best time to visit is during the week, as on weekends it sometimes gets crowded.

On the eay down, grab a snack (coxinha- a fried doughball filled with chicken) and an Açaí, and go straight to the Vidigal beach , where you can wash away the sweat with a dip into the ocean..

It is a very safe beach, and still a secret of sorts (though this is charning fast), very different to the buzzing beaches in Ipanema or Copacabana, where it is sometimes hard to relax.

Other things to do in Vidigal:

  • An amazing breakfast spot, called Café e Mar . Check out their Insta / reserving a table is necessary.
  • Bar Laje is a famous view point, that serves food and drinks, however, they charge about 80 real per person to enter, and the menu is also not the cheapest but good quality!
  • Mirante Avrao is another popular spot, for events with live music, DJs etc...

Food

Supermarkets

Vitoria mini supermarket
The closest mini supermarket, which has all the basics you might need, is only a 2-minute walk from the condominium.

Zona Sul supermarket
The best supermarket chain is called ZONA SUL. The closest branch is in Leblon at Rua Dias Ferreira 290, a pleasant 20-minute walk along the ocean. There are taxi stands in front if you’d rather take a cab back.
