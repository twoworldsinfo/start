Safety Guide for Rio de Janeiro

Food

Supermarkets

Vitoria mini supermarket
The closest mini supermarket, which has all the basics you might need, is only a 2-minute walk from the condominium.

Zona Sul supermarket
The best supermarket chain is called ZONA SUL. The closest branch is in Leblon at Rua Dias Ferreira 290, a pleasant 20-minute walk along the ocean. There are taxi stands in front if you’d rather take a cab back.

Restaurants

Breakfast Spots
• Sheraton Grand Rio Hotel & Resort breakfast buffet (7–11 am)
• Café e Mar (reservation required)
• La Bicyclette (French bakery; delivers)
• Nema (bread chain with pre-baked pizzas)
• Talho Capixaba (Leblon sandwich shop; pricey)
• Confeitaria Rio Lisboa (next door to Talho; great and affordable)
• Juice bars everywhere—look for Bibi Sucos or Natural e Sabor in Ipanema; grab “um salgado” and head to the beach.

Ice Cream & Açaí
• Borelli
• Milk Creamery
• Momo Gelato
• Gelateria Piemonte
• Açaí bowls: Oakberry

Dining Out
• Burgers Rio Ipanema
• Churrascaria: Rio Brasa Lagoa or Assador
• Teva (sophisticated vegan)
• Restaurante de quilo at Bella Blu
• Guadalupe (authentic Mexican in Botafogo)
• Parma (all-you-can-eat pizza)
• Rascal (Italian buffet in Shopping Leblon)
• Bistro inside Livraria da Travessa
• Sushi: Haru Sushi Bar, San Leblon, Pabu Izakaya
