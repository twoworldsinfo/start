Supermarkets

Vitoria mini supermarket
The closest mini supermarket, which has all the basics you might need, is only a 2-minute walk from the condominium.

Zona Sul supermarket
The best supermarket chain is called ZONA SUL. The closest branch is in Leblon at Rua Dias Ferreira 290, a pleasant 20-minute walk along the ocean. There are taxi stands in front if you’d rather take a cab back.

Taxi vs. Uber: In Rio, taxis tend to be cleaner and more reliable than Ubers. Always insist the driver turns on the meter—never accept a fixed price. To return home, say: “Estrada do Vidigal 90, Edifício Costa Azul.” If they confuse it with the favela hill entrance, reassure them: “Esse endereço não está na subida da favela; é um condomínio, por favor me leva até a porta.”

Zona Sul also offers delivery via its app/website, plus an in-store buffet/restaurant for breakfast and lunch.

Restaurants

Breakfast Spots
• Sheraton hotel buffet (7–11 am)
• Café e Mar (in the favela; reservation required)
• La Bicyclette (French bakery; delivers)
• Nema (bread chain with pre-baked pizzas)
• Talho (Leblon sandwich shop; pricey)
• Confeitaria Rio Lisboa (next to Talho; great and affordable)
• Juice bars everywhere—look for Bibi Suco or Natural e Sabor in Ipanema; grab “um salgado” and head to the beach.

Ice Cream & Açaí
• Borelli (Leblon)
• Milk Creamery (Leblon milkshakes)
• Momo, Gelateria Piemonte
• Açaí bowls: Oakberry (my favorite)

Dining Out
• Burgers Rio Ipanema (fast delivery)
• Churrascaria: Rio Brasa Lagoa (good meat), or Assador (great view, ~250 BRL/person excluding drinks)
• Teva (sophisticated vegan)
• Restaurante de quilo (pay by weight; try Bella Blu in Leblon)
• Guadalupe (authentic Mexican in Botafogo)
• Parma (all-you-can-eat pizza, < 50 BRL)
• Rascal (Italian buffet at Shopping Leblon)
• Bistro inside Livraria do Shopping Leblon
• Botecas (casual bars with cheap bites; no single standout)
• Sushi: Haru Sushi (Copacabana), San & Pabu Izakaya (Leblon)
